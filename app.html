About

Features of the ChatBot

The medical chabot functions as the primary aid to enable the patients self-diagnos themselves and look for the correct treatment across several clinics and hospitals.

  • 1:1 personal assistance to the user
  • Pretexts enabled to help user select the avaialable options for assistance
  • Speech to Text enabled to help user interact with the chatbot more uniquely

Healthcare services face a huge challenge of supply-and-demand which you can fix when you create a chatbot. An AI-enabled conversational UX can deliver personalized experiences to your patients for identifying the illness, scheduling doctor appointments, notifying caregivers about symptoms, monitoring the health status, updating the homecare assistant from time-to-time and more. The Chatbots are the computer programs that interact with the users using natural language. The chatbot stores the information in the database to identify the keywords from the sentences and make a decision for the query and answers the question.
